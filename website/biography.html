Biography

FIFTH is a rising force in the drum & bass scene, known for delivering high-energy performances and meticulously crafted productions. With a deep passion for the darker, more atmospheric side of the genre, FIFTH seamlessly blends rolling basslines with intricate sound design to create tracks that captivate both underground heads and festival crowds alike.

Drawing inspiration from the liquid and neurofunk subgenres, FIFTH's sonic palette is both diverse and distinctive. Whether it's thunderous drops that shake sound systems or smooth, melodic interludes that provide moments of reflection, each track tells a story and takes listeners on a journey through sound.

With releases gaining support from prominent DJs and radio shows across the globe, FIFTH continues to push boundaries and explore new sonic territories. From intimate club settings to massive festival stages, FIFTH brings an unmatched energy and dedication to every performance, solidifying a reputation as one of the most exciting artists to watch in the modern drum & bass landscape.
